The temple is over 100 years old. Jay and Vijay, the two sentinels made of marble catch the eye of all at the outset. The construction is so simple that all the proceedings in the temple along with the Ganesh idol can be seen even from outside. 
        The Ganesh idol is 2.2 metres tall and 1 metre wide. It is adorned with nearly 40 kilos of gold. 
                The temple conducts Daily pooja, abhisheka and the arti of the Ganesh. The temple is illuminated during the Ganesh festival. Shrimant Dagdusheth Ganpati Trust manages the temple. 
                         The temple is situated in the centre of city, local shopping market is also the nearby temple.
